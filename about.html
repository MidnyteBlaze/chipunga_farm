Premium Coffee, Macadamia & Honey from Malawi

Chipunga Farm is a premier agricultural enterprise based in Mzuzu, Malawi, specializing in high-quality coffee, macadamia nuts, and honey. With years of experience in sustainable farming, we pride ourselves on delivering the finest organic products to local and international markets.

Sustainably grown coffee with rich, distinctive flavor

Premium macadamia nuts from our orchards and outgrowers

Pure, natural honey from our apiaries

View Our Products

Our Mission

To produce the highest quality organic products while empowering local farming communities through sustainable practices and fair partnerships.

Our Vision

To become Malawi's leading exporter of premium coffee, macadamia nuts, and honey, recognized globally for exceptional quality and sustainability.

Our Values

Integrity, sustainability, community empowerment, and excellence in everything we do.
